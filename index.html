Control Spotify playback directly from Perception

Connect with Spotify

🔒 What this gives PCX access to:

✓ Play/Pause/Skip - Control your music playback
✓ Shuffle & Repeat - Toggle playback modes
✓ Volume Control - Adjust volume (desktop only)
✓ Now Playing - See current song, artist & album art
✓ Seek - Jump to any point in a song

🚫 What PCX can NOT access:

  • Your email or account details
  • Your playlists or saved music
  • Your listening history
  • Your followers or profile info
  • Any payment or subscription info
  • Ability to follow/unfollow or modify your library

How it works:

  1. Click "Connect with Spotify" above
  2. Log in to your Spotify account
  3. Spotify will show you the exact permissions - approve them
  4. Copy the token you receive
  5. Paste it into Perception's Spotify widget settings
🔐 This uses Spotify's official OAuth 2.0 with PKCE - the most secure authorization method.
Your password is never shared with PCX. You can revoke access anytime at spotify.com/account/apps
