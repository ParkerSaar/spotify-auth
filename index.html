Secure OAuth 2.0 Connection

Connect your Spotify account to control playback directly from Perception

Connect with Spotify

How to connect:

  1. Click "Connect with Spotify" above
  2. Log in with your Spotify account
  3. Review & approve the permissions
  4. Copy the token you'll receive
  5. Paste it in Perception → Spotify → "Link Spotify Account"

✓ What Perception can access:

✓ Play/Pause — Control playback
✓ Skip/Previous — Change tracks
✓ Shuffle — Toggle shuffle mode
✓ Repeat — Toggle repeat mode
✓ Volume — Adjust (desktop only)
✓ Seek — Jump in track
✓ Now Playing — Song & artist
✓ Album Art — Display artwork

✗ What Perception cannot access:

Email / account Playlists Saved songs Listening history Payment info Profile data
🔒 Your Spotify password is never shared with Perception. Uses official Spotify OAuth 2.0 with PKCE encryption.
Revoke access anytime: spotify.com/account/apps
