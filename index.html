Spotifyception

Spotify Widget for Perception.cx

Secure OAuth 2.0 Connection
Connect with Spotify

✓ What Perception can access:

✓ Playback Control — Play, pause, skip, previous
✓ Shuffle & Repeat — Toggle playback modes
✓ Volume — Adjust volume (desktop app only)
✓ Now Playing — Song, artist, album art, progress
✓ Seek — Jump to any position in a track

✗ What Perception cannot access:

  • Email or account info
  • Playlists or saved songs
  • Listening history
  • Payment info
  • Followers or profile
  • Library modifications

How to connect:

  1. Click "Connect with Spotify" above
  2. Log in with your Spotify account
  3. Review & approve the permissions
  4. Copy the token you'll receive
  5. Paste it in Perception's Spotify settings
🔒 Your Spotify password is never shared with Perception.
Uses official Spotify OAuth 2.0 with PKCE encryption.
Revoke access anytime: spotify.com/account/apps

✓ Your Refresh Token:

Final steps:

  1. Copy the token above
  2. Open Perception → Spotify tab
  3. Check "Link Spotify Account"
  4. Paste your token and click "Link"
  5. You're all set! 🎵
🔒 Token stored locally on your PC only.
To disconnect: spotify.com/account/apps → Remove "Spotifyception"
